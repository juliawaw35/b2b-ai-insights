Financial ROI You Can Expect

Conservative, realistic numbers for small B2B teams (5–30 employees) in the DACH region — not “AI hype”.

Time saved → direct cost savings

Average loaded cost per hour in DACH: €40–€70.
If each key employee saves 5–10 hours per week:

  • Team of 10 → €2,500–€5,000 saved every week
  • €10,000–€20,000 per month
  • €120,000–€240,000 per year

This is only from cutting repetitive admin work (emails, follow-ups, docs, CRM updates).

Faster processes, less overhead

Back-office (HR, ops, sales support, documentation) often costs €12,000–€25,000/month.

  • 20–40% efficiency gain = €2,400–€10,000/month saved
  • Fewer errors, less rework, fewer escalations
  • Shorter time-to-close and faster cashflow

Lost leads recovered → extra revenue

Most teams lose 30–55% of inbound leads due to slow replies and no follow-up. With AI-led first response and follow-up:

  • +5–10% conversion uplift on existing leads
  • At €2,000–€5,000 LTV → €50,000–€200,000 extra yearly revenue
  • In high-ticket deals (5–20k €) → up to €800,000 yearly

Lower IT & contractor spend

No-code AI cuts the need for expensive external development:

  • Less spend on agencies and freelancers (€80–€150/h)
  • Fewer CRM “mini-projects” at €5,000–€20,000 each
  • €5,000–€50,000 yearly saved on IT maintenance and change requests

Total realistic ROI (90–180 days)

  • Cost savings: €150,000–€400,000 per year
  • Revenue uplift: €50,000–€800,000 per year
  • IT cost reduction: €5,000–€50,000 per year

Combined realistic ROI for a small B2B team: €200,000–€1,000,000+ yearly impact once core workflows are automated.
